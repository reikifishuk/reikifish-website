FEATURED BOOK

Walking the Grey

BY REIKI FISH

A journey through stillness, truth and endurance — and the difficult ground between darkness and light.

Walking the Grey explores alienation, abuse, emotional survival and reclamation through the perspective of a father learning to remain steady when certainty, identity and family life are tested.

It is a book about what happens after the noise: when reaction gives way to reflection, pain becomes understanding, and strength no longer needs to announce itself.
