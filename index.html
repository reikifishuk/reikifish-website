Meet Andy

Author. Practitioner. Student of Human Nature.

I write, coach and speak from lived experience and applied psychology. My work helps people hold complexity, regulate emotion and find steadier footing in moments that demand more than easy answers.

For over two decades, I have worked at the edge of conflict, performance pressure and personal reinvention. The through-line has always been the same: clarity is not found by force. It is built through awareness, language and deliberate practice.

Walking the Grey is an extension of that philosophy. It is not a manifesto. It is a field guide for anyone learning to move beyond reaction and into a life led by grounded, conscious response.
